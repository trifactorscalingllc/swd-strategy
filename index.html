TikTok, Launch Plan

Your biggest untapped channel

Why now: your short vertical reels repurpose to TikTok 1:1 with zero extra filming, and TikTok's discovery engine is the fastest way to grow a young-male audience from scratch.
1 · Setup
  • Claim @slimwaistdemon (match the IG handle), switch to a Business/Creator account.
  • Bio mirrors IG: "Hardgainer Coach · Skinny→Jacked in 90 days · ⬇️ free plan". Link to your Stan Store.
2 · Formatting & Specs
  • 1080×1920 vertical. Sweet spot 9-15s (21-34s also performs).
  • Hook in the first 1 second, text + a strong opening line.
  • Captions ON (burned, like your reels). Add a trending sound low under your voice.
3 · Repurpose Workflow (IG → TikTok)
  • Post the clean export (no IG watermark, TikTok suppresses watermarked reels).
  • Re-write the caption more casual; 3-5 niche tags: #skinnytojacked #hardgainer #gymtok #fyp.
  • Film once → IG Reel → YouTube Short → TikTok. Same clip, three platforms.
4 · What's Different From IG
  • TikTok rewards raw & native over polished, and trending sounds.
  • Post more often: 1-3×/day. Volume = faster discovery.
  • Reply to comments with a video, huge reach hack.
  • Use Photo Mode for before/after carousels too.
5 · CTA on TikTok
TikTok's DM automation is weaker than IG, so drive to "comment SKINNY → I'll reply" and keep the link in bio to your Stan Store. Same offer, same keyword, just a lighter touch.
